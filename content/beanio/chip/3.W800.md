## 固件参数

- chip: W800
- flash: 1M (Bytes)
- frequency: 240MHz
- uart: 5
- spi: 1
- i2c: 1
- adc: 1
- dac: 0
- terminal: Serial1

## 启动模式

| A0  | mode       |
| --- | ---------- |
| L   | download   |
| H   | flash boot |
