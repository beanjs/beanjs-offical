## 固件参数

- chip: ESP32C3
- flash: 4M (Bytes)
- frequency: 240MHz
- uart: 2
- spi: 1
- i2c: 1
- adc: 2
- dac: 0
- terminal: Serial1

## 烧写参数

- chip esp32c3
- flash_freq: 40m
- flash_mode: dio
- flash_size: detect

## 烧写地址

- bootloader.bin: 0x1000
- partition-table.bin: 0x8000
- beanio.bin: 0x10000

## 启动模式

| D15 | D2  | D0  | mode     |
| --- | --- | --- | -------- |
| L   | H   | L   | download |
| L   | H   | H   | spi boot |
